Welcome back, Platform Management

This dashboard allows you to manage FSA categories and monitor platform performance through daily, weekly, and monthly reporting. You can organize activity types and review operational trends from the menu on the left.

FSA Category Functions

  • Create FSA categories to organize different types of activities
  • View FSA categories to see the list of existing categories
  • Update FSA categories to modify category names or details
  • Suspend FSA categories to disable categories that are no longer needed
  • Search FSA categories to find specific categories quickly

Reporting Functions

  • Get daily reports to monitor daily platform performance
  • Get weekly reports to analyze weekly trends
  • Get monthly reports to review long-term growth and data

Platform Management Responsibilities

As platform management, you are responsible for maintaining the category structure of fundraising service activities and reviewing operational reports over different time periods. Use the left navigation to manage categories and access reporting tools.
